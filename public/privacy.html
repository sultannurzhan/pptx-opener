Privacy First

Your presentations stay on your device

Nexuslides was built to keep slide content local. File processing, parsing, and rendering happen entirely in your browser runtime.

What stays local

.pptx binary content

The selected file is read locally via browser APIs and is not uploaded to an application server.

Extracted slide XML and media

OOXML components are unpacked and transformed in-memory inside your tab session.

Rendered slide output

Generated slide nodes are created in the DOM and remain local to your browser environment.

Analytics transparency

Nexuslides includes Firebase Analytics initialization for aggregate product metrics. This does not transmit your uploaded slide file contents.

Deck content exclusion

Presentation text, images, and OOXML internals are not intentionally sent as analytics payloads.

Operational telemetry

Analytics may include high-level usage events and device/browser metadata per Firebase defaults.

Recommended usage

For highly sensitive environments, run Nexuslides in trusted browsers and review your network and extension policies.
